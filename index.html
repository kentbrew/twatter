Remember when you could roll your own Twitter timelines? You still can.

  1. Build your Twitter widget here:
    https://twitter.com/settings/widgets/new/search
    ... and note your Twitter id. Mine is 418760697137225728.
  2. Put this in your page, where you want your tweets to show up:
    <ul id="canHazTweets"></ul>
  3. Call the script like this:
    <script src="twatter.js" data-structure-id="canHazTweets" data-widget-id="418760697137225728"></script>
